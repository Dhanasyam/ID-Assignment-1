Education

I was born in Kerala, India. I studied in India for 12 years in a school called Bhavan's Vidhyashram. But I always wanted to explore new country, new culture, new people and didn’t want to be stuck in India forever. So, I went to study in Singapore as my father works there. So, I joined a Polytechnic called Ngee Ann Polytechnic and started my IT course. The only problem that I faced was communication, as I was in a new country people do not understand me and vice versa. But I managed to be a part of Singapore and made some friends.

The only disappointment is that I didn’t get a friend who comes from the same country as me. Now I am doing the second semester in Ngee Ann Polytechnic, and it is awesome. The campus was amazing and my classmates and teachers were really welcoming. After I get my diploma, I plan to take my National Service (NS). The National service should be taken by all the Permanent residents and citizens in Singapore, so as I was a PR, I also have to do it. When my NS is done, I plan to take Computer Science in any University. My Ambition is to become a Software Engineer.
